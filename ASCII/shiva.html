Reasons to Join Clubs

Studying in college can be challenging. With an enormous amount of classes, seminars, lecture tutorials, and exams, life can be harsh and lonely. You may find yourself away from the community, friends, and social groups. These feelings can overwhelm you, and you struggle in making new friends. However, if you join a club, the process of making friends is not something that difficult.
